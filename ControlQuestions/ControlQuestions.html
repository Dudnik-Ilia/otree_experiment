In this experiment, you will provide your estimate for the probability of a specific event occurring. We use the Randomized Scoring Rule mechanism. The probability you provide will influence your payout: the payout mechanism is designed so that the more truthful your estimate is, the more chances of getting better payout.

Here how it works:

  • You will state the probability Y (e.g., 50%) that you believe France will become the European champion.
  • The computer randomly selects a number X between 0 and 100.
    • If Y ≥ X, you will receive additional payout if France actually becomes champion.
    • If Y < X, you will win additional payout with a probability of X%, regardless of whether France wins.

Why is this mechanism used? This payout method ensures it is always in your best interest to truthfully report your belief.
