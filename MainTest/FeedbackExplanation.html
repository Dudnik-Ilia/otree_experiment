In the end of the next 2 rounds of Credit Assessment tasks, you will receive Messages about your relative performance that will be delivered to you. Right after them you will have the opportunity to update your estimate (make a new guess) of your performance each time.

These messages will be delivered by 1 of 3 messengers, as shown in the image. However, not all messengers are trustworthy. While 2 messengers always deliver the truth, 1 messenger will always provide false information about your performance. For each message, a messenger will be randomly chosen, and you will not know which one was chosen.



This means that each time, there is a 66% probability of receiving a true message and a 33% probability of receiving a false message about your actual performance. Note that, it is also possible to receive both false messages.

After each message, you will have another chance to estimate how likely it is that you performed better than the AI. Note: You maximize your payout by providing your most accurate estimate.
