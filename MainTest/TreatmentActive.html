Motivation for the study

We as a company are at a critical juncture: the decision to integrate AI systems to replace low-level managers. If we determine that AI significantly outperforms human decision-making, around 1,000 managers at our branches are going to be replaced by an automated system.

However, there is a concern that losing unique human knowledge might disrupt decision-making processes and lead to unforeseen challenges. All this has led to organizing this experiment.
